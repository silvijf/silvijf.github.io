Platformer Game
You are a guy with super kicking legs. You will notice. But you are stranded in a world with a blue background. Luckily you find platforms. You are the Platformer! You can move with A, D and arrow keys. Every other key is jump!
Canvas
Here you can draw stuff. Try to save and load on the 2 slots. Trick: To get better colors, save your creation and load it a few times. Why don't you try to set the color to #ffffff?
Race clicker
Be ready, because a super clicking car experience is waiting on you! Tip: use every click key you have! (If you have a touchpad, it may have 2 or 3.) This game may be hard is you just have 1 click button. Oh, and click on clicks. They help you!
Copy Hub
If you want to copy something, and then copy something else, and then want to copy the first one, you can use this tool! Did you know that you can use CTRL + Z and CTRL + Y?
Is it I?
Is it I? Is it l? Is it |? Is it 1? You can test it with this tool. Just copy it and paste it in the box. Do you know what | would be (without looking at what you just read)?
